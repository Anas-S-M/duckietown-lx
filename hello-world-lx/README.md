# **Learning Experience (LX): Hello World**

# About these activities

This set of **hello world** activities will guide you through the workflow used to progress through every Duckietown Learning Experience.

After completing this LX, you will have all the skills you need to successfully work through any Duckietown Learning Experience. Jump into an LX from the [Duckietown Learning Experiences directory](https://staging-docs.duckietown.com/daffy-staging/devmanual-lx/consume/3_next/index.html) to start expanding your knowledge of robotic systems, building behaviors for your Duckiebot, and more.

# How do I complete this Learning Experience?

Follow the instructions in the [Duckietown Learning Experience Developer Manual](https://staging-docs.duckietown.com/daffy-staging/devmanual-lx/intro.html) to progress through this LX.  You will need to complete the following sections:


## Step 1: Set Up Your Environment

Preparation is key! Follow the [Environment Setup guide](https://staging-docs.duckietown.com/daffy-staging/devmanual-lx/consume/1_setup/index.html) to prepare your development tools.

## Step 2: Try Each Activity

Now that you have all the necessary tools, follow the [`dts code` Workflow guide](https://staging-docs.duckietown.com/daffy-staging/devmanual-lx/consume/2_instructions/index.html) to walk through the activities contained in this learning experience.

## Step 3: Explore!

Use your new skills to explore all of the available [Duckietown Learning Experiences](https://staging-docs.duckietown.com/daffy-staging/devmanual-lx/consume/3_next/index.html).  Be sure to connect with the rest of the [Duckietown community on Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/duckietown/shared_invite/enQtNTU0Njk4NzU2NTY1LWM2YzdlNmJmOTg4MzAyODc2YTI3YTc5MzE2MThkZGUwYTFkZWQ4M2ZlZGU1YTZhYjg5YTgzNDkyMzI2ZjNhZWE) to find support, be inspired by other developer's work, and share your own Duckietown projects with community members around the world.
